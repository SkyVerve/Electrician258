How often should I have my smoke detectors inspected?

It's recommended to have your smoke detectors inspected by a professional at least once a year to ensure they are in good working condition.

What will you check during the inspection?

The inspection includes checking the battery, wiring, placement, functionality, and compliance with local fire codes.

How long does an inspection take?

A typical inspection takes 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the number of detectors in your home or business.

What if my smoke detector isn't working?

If a detector is malfunctioning, our licensed electricians can repair or replace faulty units to ensure your safety.

Are professional inspections required by law?

In many areas, local building codes require that smoke detectors be inspected and maintained regularly by qualified professionals.
