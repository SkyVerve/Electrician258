What are the signs that my electrical panel needs repair?

Common signs include frequently tripping breakers, flickering lights, a burning smell from the panel, or visible signs of rust and corrosion.

How long does a panel repair take?

Most repairs can be completed within a few hours, depending on the complexity of the issue.

Can I repair the panel myself?

Due to the high risk of electric shock and fire, all electrical panel repairs should be performed by a licensed professional.

Will you need to shut off my power?

Yes, for safety reasons, we will need to temporarily shut off the power to your home or business while we work on the panel.

Do you offer emergency panel repair services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent electrical issues, including panel repairs.
