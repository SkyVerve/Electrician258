What is a Level 2 charger?

A Level 2 charger uses a 240-volt outlet (like an electric dryer) and can charge an EV significantly faster than a standard 120-volt Level 1 charger.

Will I need to upgrade my electrical panel?

It depends on your current electrical system's capacity. We will perform a load calculation to determine if an upgrade is necessary to safely handle the new charger.

How long does the installation take?

A typical installation takes between 2 to 4 hours, depending on the complexity and location of the charger.

Can you install any brand of EV charger?

Yes, our licensed electricians are experienced in installing all major brands, including Tesla, ChargePoint, JuiceBox, and others.
