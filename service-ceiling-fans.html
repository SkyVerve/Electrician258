Ceiling Fan Installation

Stylish and efficient ceiling fan installation, repair, and maintenance for optimal comfort and airflow in any room.

Schedule an Installation
  • Professional Installation: Secure mounting and wiring for all fan types, including those with lights and remote controls.
  • Smart Fan Integration: Setup and configuration for smart home-compatible fans that can be controlled by voice or app.
  • Balancing and Noise Reduction: We ensure your fan is perfectly balanced for smooth, quiet, and wobble-free operation.
  • Improved Energy Efficiency: Ceiling fans can reduce your reliance on air conditioning, lowering energy bills in the summer.
  • Enhanced Room Aesthetics: A modern ceiling fan can be a stylish centerpiece that complements your room's decor.
  • Safety and Reliability: Professional installation ensures the fan is securely mounted and wired correctly, eliminating risks of falling or electrical faults.

Can you install a fan where there is no existing light fixture?

Yes, we can run new wiring and install a fan-rated electrical box to mount a ceiling fan in a new location.

How long does it take to install a ceiling fan?

A standard installation replacing an existing fixture typically takes 1-2 hours. New installations may take longer.

My fan is wobbling. Can you fix it?

Absolutely. Wobbling is usually caused by imbalanced blades or a loose mounting. We can diagnose and fix the issue to ensure smooth operation.

Do I need a special wall switch for a fan with a light?

It depends on your desired control. We can install dual switches, dimmer controls, or set up remote/smart control systems to operate the fan and light independently.
